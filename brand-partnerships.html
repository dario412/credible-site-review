Our position

Why we say no to most.

We turn away ~80% of inbound ambassador enquiries. The reason is boring but structural: signed creators can only credibly hold two ambassador roles at a time. Any more and the affiliation reads as noise instead of endorsement.

When we say yes, we mean it. That means we're rigorous on category fit, values alignment, and the specifics of what "brand ambassador" actually asks of the creator over 18 months.

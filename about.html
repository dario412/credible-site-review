About Credible

Talent management, rebuilt for the people who shape B2B.

Credible was founded in 2026 by the team at PepTalk — the marketplace for human intelligence — to solve a specific gap in the market: the founders, operators and specialists driving B2B conversation didn't have representation built for the businesses they were actually running.

The story

Why we exist.

PepTalk has spent the last decade connecting businesses with the human intelligence they need — from world-class speakers to specialist practitioners. Over that time we noticed something changing on both sides of that transaction.

The people businesses wanted to hear from were no longer the professional keynote circuit. They were operators publishing daily, founders running media businesses on the side, investors whose writing shaped markets.

And the people buying that talent were changing too. New in-house roles at companies like Perplexity, Anthropic and Notion. Specialist creator agencies like Creator Match and Creator Buzz briefing on behalf of their brand rosters. A whole buyer-side category emerging without the supply-side infrastructure to serve it properly.

So we built it. Credible is the talent management arm — signing a selective roster of B2B expert creators, and working direct with brands and agencies who need to reach the audiences those creators already have.

Part of PepTalk

A PepTalk company.

Credible is a business inside the PepTalk group. PepTalk is the marketplace for human intelligence — the platform businesses use to find speakers, practitioners and experts for their events, content and strategic work.

Where PepTalk is the marketplace, Credible is the management arm. We handle representation, career-building and business management for a selective roster of expert creators. The two businesses share buyer relationships, but our talent commitments are ours alone.
